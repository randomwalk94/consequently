<p>The <a href="http://rspas.anu.edu.au/ir/">Department of International Relations</a> at the Australian National University's <a href="http://rspas.anu.edu.au">Research School of Pacific and Asian Studies</a> publishes a lot of <a href="http://rspas.anu.edu.au/ir/publications.html">interesting material</a>.  Their "keynotes series" are intended for the general public.  Keynote 1 is 
<a href="http://rspas.anu.edu.au/ir/keynotes/keynotes-01.htm">The Day the World Changed? Terrorism and the World Order</a>, and Keynote 2 is <a href="http://rspas.anu.edu.au/ir/keynotes/keynotes-02.htm">Refugees and the Myth of the Borderless World</a>.</p>
